EVIE F1 (Developing)

EVEI F1 is an electric front cargo bike that is implemented with a unique steering system along with suspension installed in it. This is the project where I contribute most of my interest, skills, techniques into it. Let me show you why.


This is a sample cargo bike, where it requires a head tube to achieve a normal bike steering and turning motion.

This is Kettler Cargoline Series, where it doen't require headtube to achieved turning motion with an unique steering mechanism. Hence, my challenges are:

  • Research and design the steering system and suspension system to achieve similar results to conquer bike structure weakness
  • Selection and design of critical components (dropout, forging hinge, rocker, torsion bar, tube size, bearing and others)
  • Appearance of overall bike to meet market and design requirements

Key outputs:
  • Collected steering ratio data based on design requirements through DOE
  • Suspension system was proposed and designed under the chassis (vertical space of 80mm, which is the height of the tube)
  • Collected market's bike structure dimension and finalised the dimension required
  • Overall bike appearance with functional components were designed and ready for overview and manufactured

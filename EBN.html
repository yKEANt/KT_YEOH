Edible Bird Nest (EBN) Cleaning Machine

The EBN Cleaning Machine in Solidworks

This is an Integrated Design Project (IDP) group assignment where students need to research and design a industrial related machine to fullfill the cours requirements. Recently, automation in agriculture and traditional sector has a booming scenario where many traditional works have been replace with automated machines due to its repeatedly process and actions. Edible Bird Nest (EBN) is a huge potential market where nowadays the industry still rely on human labour to complete the cleaning tasks.
Hence, my teammate has proposed a cleaning method to solve this issues as shown below. We have reached out one of the EBN industry, the OAS Bird Net Industry to understand the EBN industry in terms of its challenges, process and market potential.

CONCLUSION

Due to the tiem constraint, we only able to research, design and analyse our proposed design based on the resources we have. The experiments has brought out an expected outcome, where the EBN is able to be cleaned but still need to undergo hand-picking cleaning process. It is challenging to clean the EBN up to 100% by only go through 1 cleaning process. Some simulation were done on our design too. It's a nice experience to conduct a research and design projects based on the market needs.

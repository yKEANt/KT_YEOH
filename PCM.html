Pole Climbing Mechanism (PCM)


PCM is a mechanism that designed to move along a tube or pipe by carrying payload on it and controlled by phone. The payload indicates the tools installed on the mechanism to perform dangerous tasks (inspection, reparing and delivering) at high attitude.

The PCM can be categorized into active and passive parts, which indicates the motion on each parts. The active parts was installed with an actuators and the electrical system . Passive part contribute extra force to hold on the pole.
FBD was drawn and analysed to perform calculation on force required to move along the pole. calculation such as torque requirements, speed, tube bending, friction force were performed.

The PCM was fabricated using aluminium AL6061 material, while the customized components were 3D printed. FEA analysis was performed on the 3D printed parts to inspect the critical failure points, displacement, stress distribution, FOS and other useful information to undergo modification.

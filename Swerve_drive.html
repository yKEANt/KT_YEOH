Swerve Drive

Self-developed robotics project @ UMS Robotics Club

Swerve drive, an AGV system where it can provide higher thrust force compare with omniwheel and mecanum wheel. It's consists of 2-DOF. The thrust force of wheel will be directed towards the moving direction of the wheel-base.

Motors: BLDC (thrust) and DC (Direction)
Gear for thrust: Timing belt pulley (1:1 ratio)
Gear for direction: 3D print spur gear (1:2 ratio)
Sensors: Absolute encoder (to identify current wheel angle)
Wheel : PU Wheel/Solid Rubber wheel
Completion: 65%

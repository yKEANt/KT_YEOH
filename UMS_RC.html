In this section showcase my enrollment in UMS Robotics Club. Robotics Club has build me a strong fundamentals in designing mechanism and robotics project. It provides as a platform for the students to learn and join robotics related events.

UMS RC Batch 2022/23


ROBOCON 2021

With limited materials, time, members and without any training before, this is our first time joining the national competition. Hence, we were exploring the techniques, equipments and skills on designing and prototyping the robots. But the most important is, learn to communicate with each other.

A turnable-pneumatic hand gripper is designed to grab the arrow. After the arrow has been turned 360 degree, it will be passed to the thrower.







Unfortunately, the Robocon 2021 was cancelled due to the COVID-19 pandemic. But it was a great experience for us, a group of people without any experience to learn all the necessary techniques in building a robot,


ROBOCON 2022

And YES, FINALLY! Our first time joining the national robotics competition, the Robocon 2022! We have accomplished our first competition where we have successfully designed, fabricated our robots. We disassembly, packed it in boxes, flied through the South China Sea to Penang and assembly the robot again. Yeap, a hectic journey but unavoidable proces for the only team from Borneo.

Our Marci (left) and Stellar (right)!


Marci, is a shooter robot where it has installed with 2 motor shooter to shoot the ball towards the target. It has a gripper hand to grab the ball and pass to shooter.





Let's talk about Stellar. Stellar's most iconic design is it has a hand gripper like a crab to grab the target (Lagori). It is installed on a turntable bearing to achiece a rotational movement, with a liting mechanism. Hence, it is a more complicated robot than Marci.





Besides, it has a similar hand gripper as Marci to grab the ball and pass to Marci.


In this competition, it was a wonderful experience. Even though we didn't achieve what we have targeted, but we have achieved what we didn't expected. Something more valueable.
